Privacy Policy for OpaqueAI Privacy Guard

Last updated: January 4, 2025

1. Introduction

OpaqueAI Privacy Guard ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our extension handles information.

2. Information Collection

Our extension:

  • Does NOT collect or store any user data
  • Does NOT transmit any information to external servers
  • Processes all text analysis locally within your browser
  • Does NOT track your browsing history or behavior

3. How It Works

The extension operates entirely locally on your device:

  • Scans text input only on supported AI platform websites
  • Performs pattern matching to identify potentially sensitive information
  • Displays warnings through local browser notifications

4. Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API solely for saving user preferences. No sensitive information is ever stored.

5. Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

6. Updates

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices. Users will be notified of any material changes.
